Apa itu Direct & Indirect Speech?
Melaporkan ucapan orang lain

Direct speech (kalimat langsung) mengutip persis kata-kata seseorang dengan tanda kutip. Indirect speech (kalimat tidak langsung / reported speech) melaporkan ucapan tersebut tanpa tanda kutip, dengan perubahan tenses, pronoun, dan keterangan waktu.

Direct Speech
She said, "I am very happy."
→ Dia berkata, "Saya sangat bahagia."
Indirect Speech (Reported Speech)
She said that she was very happy.
→ Dia mengatakan bahwa dia sangat bahagia.
💡
3 Perubahan Utama: Saat mengubah direct → indirect, perhatikan tiga hal: 1) Tenses mundur satu langkah, 2) Pronoun berubah, 3) Keterangan waktu/tempat berubah.
?
Perubahan Tenses
Tenses mundur satu langkah (backshift)

Ini adalah aturan terpenting dalam indirect speech. Ketika reporting verb (said, told, asked) dalam past tense, tenses dalam kutipan mundur satu langkah.

Direct Speech → Indirect Speech
Simple Present (am/is) ? Simple Past (was/were)
Present Continuous (is doing) ? Past Continuous (was doing)
Simple Past (did) ? Past Perfect (had done)
Present Perfect (has done) ? Past Perfect (had done)
will ? would
can ? could
may ? might
must ? had to
⚠️
Pengecualian! Past Perfect TIDAK berubah. "I had finished" tetap "I had finished" di indirect speech. Begitu juga could, would, should, might — sudah di bentuk past, tidak berubah lagi.
👤
Perubahan Pronoun & Keterangan Waktu
Menyesuaikan sudut pandang dan waktu

Selain tenses, pronoun dan keterangan waktu/tempat juga berubah agar sesuai dengan sudut pandang pelapor.

Perubahan Pronoun
👤
I ? he/she
"I am happy" → She said she was happy.
📚
we → they
"We are ready" → They said they were ready.
📚
my → his/her
"My book" ? He said it was his book.
🔑
you ? I/he/she/they
Tergantung siapa "you" merujuk.
Perubahan Keterangan Waktu
Direct → Indirect
today ? that day
yesterday ? the day before / the previous day
tomorrow ? the next day / the following day
now ? then / at that time
here ? there
this ? that
ago ? before / earlier

Reported Questions
Melaporkan pertanyaan dalam indirect speech

Pertanyaan dalam indirect speech tidak menggunakan tanda tanya dan mengikuti susunan kalimat pernyataan (S + V). Gunakan "if/whether" untuk yes/no questions dan kata tanya (what, why, dll.) untuk WH-questions.

Yes/No Question
Direct: "Do you like coffee?"
Indirect: He asked if I liked coffee.
WH-Question
Direct: "Where do you live?"
Indirect: She asked where I lived.
📚
Urutan kata tidak dibalik! Di reported question, susunan jadi S + V (seperti pernyataan), bukan V + S. "Where do you live?" ? "...where I lived" ? BUKAN "...where did I live" ?.
📢
Reported Commands & Requests
Melaporkan perintah dan permintaan

Untuk perintah dan permintaan, gunakan told/asked + object + to + infinitive.

Command (Perintah)
Direct: "Sit down!"
Indirect: The teacher told us to sit down.
Negative Command (Larangan)
Direct: "Don't be late!"
Indirect: She told me not to be late.
Request (Permintaan)
Direct: "Could you help me, please?"
Indirect: He asked me to help him.
📚
Tips & Jebakan Soal SNBT
Strategi menjawab soal reported speech
1
Hafalkan tabel backshift. Ini kunci utama. Setiap tenses mundur satu langkah kecuali Past Perfect yang tetap.
2
"Said" vs "Told". Said tidak diikuti object langsung (said that...). Told wajib diikuti object (told me/him/her that...). "He said me..." ❌ = SALAH.
3
Reported questions ? urutan S + V. Pastikan susunan kalimat indirect question tidak dibalik seperti pertanyaan asli.
4
"If" vs "Whether". Keduanya bisa dipakai di reported yes/no questions. "Whether" lebih formal dan bisa diikuti "or not".
5
Jika reporting verb dalam present tense, tenses TIDAK berubah. "She says, 'I am happy'" ? "She says she is happy." Backshift hanya terjadi jika reporting verb past tense.

Struktur Email Formal
Komponen wajib email profesional

Email formal memiliki 5 komponen utama yang harus ada. Setiap bagian harus profesional, jelas, dan langsung ke inti.

📚
Subject Line
Jelas dan spesifik. "Meeting Reschedule — March 10" ✅ bukan "Hi" ?
📚
Greeting / Salutation
Formal: Dear Mr./Ms. [Name] atau Dear Sir/Madam jika tidak tahu nama.
📝
Body
Paragraf 1: tujuan. Paragraf 2: detail. Paragraf 3: call to action.
📚
Closing
Sincerely, Best regards, Yours faithfully — sesuaikan formalitas.
💼
Template: Email Lamaran Kerja
Job application email
To: hr@company.com
Subject: Application for Marketing Coordinator Position — Ref: MC-2024

Dear Ms. Johnson,

I am writing to express my interest in the Marketing Coordinator position advertised on your company website. With three years of experience in digital marketing and a proven track record of increasing social media engagement by 150%, I believe I would be a valuable addition to your team.

I have attached my resume and portfolio for your review. I am available for an interview at your earliest convenience and can be reached at (021) 123-4567 or via this email.

Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to the opportunity to discuss how my skills align with your team's goals.

Sincerely,
Ahmad Rizki
Digital Marketing Specialist

📩
Template: Email Permintaan Informasi
Inquiry email
To: admissions@university.edu
Subject: Inquiry About Scholarship Opportunities for International Students

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am a prospective international student from Indonesia, and I am writing to inquire about scholarship opportunities available for the upcoming academic year. I am particularly interested in the Computer Science program and would like to know the eligibility criteria and application deadlines.

Could you also kindly provide information regarding accommodation options and estimated living costs? Any brochures or links to relevant resources would be greatly appreciated.

Thank you for your time and assistance. I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours faithfully,
Sari Dewi

📚
"Dear Sir or Madam" + "Yours faithfully" — Ini pasangan yang benar ketika tidak mengetahui nama penerima. Jika tahu nama: "Dear Mr. Smith" + "Yours sincerely".
📚
Formal vs Informal
Perbedaan tone dan bahasa

Pilihan kata dan gaya bahasa harus sesuai dengan situasi dan hubungan dengan penerima.

👔
Formal
I would like to inquire...
Please find attached...
I would appreciate it if...
I look forward to hearing from you.
👕
Informal
Just wondering...
Here's the file...
It would be great if...
Hope to hear from you soon!
📚
Hindari: Singkatan (don't → do not), emoji, bahasa slang, dan kalimat terlalu pendek dalam email formal. Juga hindari MENULIS DENGAN HURUF KAPITAL SEMUA — ini terasa seperti berteriak.
🎯
Tips Menulis Email Efektif
Prinsip-prinsip penting
1
Subject line harus spesifik. "Meeting" ❌ → "Q2 Budget Meeting — Thursday 3PM" ✅. Subject line yang baik meningkatkan kemungkinan email dibaca.
2
Langsung ke inti di paragraf pertama. Nyatakan tujuan emailmu di kalimat pertama atau kedua. Jangan bertele-tele.
3
Satu email = satu topik. Jika ada topik lain, kirim email terpisah. Ini memudahkan follow-up dan pelacakan.
4
Proofread sebelum kirim. Cek nama penerima (salah nama = fatal!), attachment, ejaan, dan tone keseluruhan.
5
Closing yang tepat. "Yours faithfully" untuk orang yang tidak dikenal. "Yours sincerely" / "Best regards" untuk yang sudah dikenal.

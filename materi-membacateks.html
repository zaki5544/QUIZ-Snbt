Strategi Membaca Efektif
Cara memahami teks bahasa Inggris dengan cepat

Membaca teks bahasa Inggris bukan berarti harus menerjemahkan setiap kata. Dengan strategi yang tepat, kamu bisa memahami inti teks tanpa kamus.

📚
Skimming
Baca secara cepat untuk mendapat gambaran umum. Perhatikan judul, kalimat pertama tiap paragraf, dan kata kunci.
🎯
Scanning
Cari informasi spesifik seperti nama, angka, atau kata tertentu tanpa membaca seluruh teks.
📚
Context Clues
Tebak arti kata dari konteks kalimat. Perhatikan kata-kata di sekitarnya sebagai petunjuk.
📝
Main Idea
Identifikasi ide utama setiap paragraf. Biasanya ada di kalimat pertama atau terakhir paragraf.
💡
Pro tip: Baca pertanyaan DULU sebelum membaca teks. Ini membantu otakmu fokus mencari informasi yang dibutuhkan saat membaca.

Teks 1: My Daily Routine
Teks tentang rutinitas harian
Easy
My Daily Routine
My name is Rina. I am a student at a senior high school in Jakarta. Every morning, I wake up at 5 o'clock. First, I take a shower and get dressed. Then, I have breakfast with my family. My mother usually cooks rice and eggs for us.

I go to school by bus. The bus stop is near my house, so I only walk for five minutes. School starts at 7 AM and finishes at 2 PM. After school, I usually go to the library to study or read books.

In the evening, I help my mother cook dinner. After dinner, I do my homework and watch TV for a while. I go to bed at 9:30 PM because I need enough sleep to stay healthy.
Pertanyaan Pemahaman
Pertanyaan 1
What time does Rina wake up?
✅ Jawaban B. Dalam teks disebutkan "I wake up at 5 o'clock."
Pertanyaan 2
How does Rina go to school?
? Jawaban C. Teks menyebutkan "I go to school by bus."
Pertanyaan 3
What does Rina usually do after school?
✅ Jawaban A. "After school, I usually go to the library to study or read books."

Teks 2: The Importance of Water
Teks informatif tentang air
Easy
The Importance of Water
Water is one of the most important things for all living beings. Humans, animals, and plants all need water to survive. Our bodies are made up of about 60 percent water.

We use water every day for many activities such as drinking, cooking, bathing, and washing clothes. Farmers also need water to grow their crops. Without water, plants cannot grow, and there will be no food for us to eat.

Unfortunately, clean water is becoming scarce in some parts of the world. Pollution from factories and households contaminates rivers and lakes. We must save water and keep our water sources clean. Simple actions like turning off the tap while brushing teeth can make a big difference.
Pertanyaan Pemahaman
Pertanyaan 1
How much of our body is made up of water?
✅ Jawaban B. Teks menyebutkan "Our bodies are made up of about 60 percent water."
Pertanyaan 2
What does the word "scarce" most likely mean?
✅ Jawaban C. "Scarce" = langka/sedikit. Konteks: "clean water is becoming scarce" — air bersih menjadi langka.
Pertanyaan 3
What is one simple way to save water mentioned in the text?
✅ Jawaban D. Teks menyebutkan "turning off the tap while brushing teeth can make a big difference."

Teks 3: A Visit to the Museum
Teks naratif tentang kunjungan ke museum
Medium
A Visit to the Museum
Last Saturday, my class went on a field trip to the National Museum. We took a bus from school and arrived at the museum at 9 AM. Our teacher, Mr. Budi, told us to stay together and pay attention to the guide.

The museum had many interesting exhibits. We saw ancient weapons, traditional clothing from different regions, and a collection of historical artifacts from the colonial era. My favorite part was the dinosaur fossil display on the second floor. I was amazed to see how huge dinosaurs were millions of years ago.

After the tour, we had lunch in the museum's garden. My friend Andi said that he wanted to become an archaeologist someday. I thought that was a cool idea. We returned to school at 3 PM, feeling tired but happy. It was one of the most memorable school trips I have ever had.
Pertanyaan Pemahaman
Pertanyaan 1
When did the class go to the museum?
? Jawaban A. Kalimat pertama: "Last Saturday, my class went on a field trip to the National Museum."
Pertanyaan 2
What was the writer's favorite exhibit?
✅ Jawaban B. "My favorite part was the dinosaur fossil display on the second floor."
Pertanyaan 3
What does Andi want to be in the future?
? Jawaban C. "My friend Andi said that he wanted to become an archaeologist someday."

Tips Membaca untuk SNBT
Strategi menjawab soal reading comprehension
1
Baca pertanyaan dulu. Sebelum membaca teks, lihat dulu pertanyaannya. Ini membuat otakmu "tahu apa yang dicari" saat membaca.
2
Jangan terjemahkan kata per kata. Fokus pada pemahaman umum. Kamu tidak perlu memahami setiap kata untuk menjawab pertanyaan.
3
Perhatikan kata kunci di pertanyaan. Who, what, when, where, why, how — kata-kata ini memberi petunjuk tentang informasi yang harus dicari.
4
Cari jawaban di teks, bukan dari asumsi. Jawaban yang benar selalu didukung oleh informasi yang ada di teks. Jangan menebak dari pengetahuan umum.
5
Perhatikan sinonim. Jawaban di pilihan ganda sering menggunakan kata yang berbeda tapi artinya sama dengan yang ada di teks (parafrase).

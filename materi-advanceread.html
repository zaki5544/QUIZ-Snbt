Teknik Membaca Kritis
Melampaui pemahaman literal

Membaca kritis berarti tidak sekadar memahami apa yang tertulis, tapi juga menganalisis mengapa penulis menulisnya, bagaimana argumen dibangun, dan apa implikasinya.

🎯
Identify Main Idea
Temukan thesis statement atau klaim utama penulis — biasanya di paragraf pertama atau terakhir.
📚
Trace the Argument
Ikuti bagaimana penulis membangun argumen: claim ? evidence ? conclusion.
🧠
Make Inferences
Tarik kesimpulan dari informasi yang tersirat (implied), bukan hanya tersurat (stated).
📚
Detect Tone & Purpose
Kenali nada penulis: persuasive, informative, critical, satirical.

Teks 1: The Ethics of AI in Education
Teks argumentatif — analisis perspektif penulis
The Ethics of Artificial Intelligence in Education

As artificial intelligence increasingly permeates educational institutions, a fundamental question arises: does AI enhance learning, or does it merely create an illusion of understanding? Proponents argue that AI-powered tutoring systems can provide personalized learning experiences, adapting to each student's pace and identifying knowledge gaps with unprecedented accuracy.

However, critics contend that over-reliance on AI may erode critical thinking skills — the very competencies that education seeks to develop. When students receive instant answers rather than struggling through problems, they risk losing the cognitive resilience that comes from intellectual perseverance. Moreover, the algorithms driving these systems often reflect the biases of their creators, potentially perpetuating inequalities rather than addressing them.

The solution likely lies not in choosing between human educators and AI, but in thoughtfully integrating both. Technology should serve as a tool that amplifies human teaching, not a substitute that diminishes it.

📚
Analisis: Penulis menggunakan balanced perspective — menyajikan pro dan kontra sebelum memberikan kesimpulan. Nada (tone) = analytical/balanced. Tujuan (purpose) = to persuade readers toward a balanced approach.

Teks 2: Ocean Plastic Crisis
Teks eksposisi — identifikasi struktur argumen
The Invisible Threat: Microplastics in Our Oceans

Every minute, the equivalent of one garbage truck of plastic waste enters the ocean. While large plastic debris has received considerable media attention, the more insidious threat comes from microplastics — fragments smaller than five millimeters that have infiltrated virtually every marine ecosystem on Earth.

Recent studies have detected microplastics in the deepest ocean trenches, in Arctic ice cores, and, most alarmingly, in the bodies of marine organisms consumed by humans. The implications for human health remain poorly understood, but preliminary research suggests potential links to endocrine disruption and chronic inflammation.

What makes this crisis particularly challenging is its irreversibility. Unlike many pollutants, plastic does not biodegrade — it simply breaks into ever-smaller pieces, accumulating in the environment indefinitely. Addressing this crisis demands systemic change: reducing plastic production, redesigning packaging, and investing in biodegradable alternatives.

📚
Analisis: Struktur = Problem ? Evidence ? Solution. Penulis menggunakan statistik ("every minute, one garbage truck") untuk dramatic effect. Kata "insidious" menunjukkan nada urgent/alarming.

Teks 3: Remote Work Revolution
Teks diskursif — evaluasi dan inferensi
The Remote Work Paradox

The pandemic accelerated a transformation that had been slowly unfolding for decades: the shift from office-centric work to location-independent productivity. For many professionals, working from home represented a liberation — no commute, flexible hours, and the ability to design one's own work environment.

Yet research paints a more complicated picture. While individual productivity often increases in remote settings, organizational creativity and spontaneous collaboration tend to decline. The informal hallway conversations, random encounters at coffee machines, and impromptu brainstorming sessions that once sparked innovation are difficult to replicate in scheduled video calls.

Perhaps the most profound impact has been on younger workers who entered the workforce remotely. Without the informal mentoring and cultural immersion that physical offices provide, many report feeling disconnected, professionally stunted, and uncertain about career trajectories. The challenge for organizations is clear: how to preserve the benefits of flexibility while rebuilding the human connections that remote work inadvertently severed.

📚
Inferensi: Meskipun penulis tidak secara eksplisit mengatakan "remote work is bad", kita bisa menyimpulkan bahwa penulis mendukung model hybrid — kata "paradox" dan frasa "more complicated picture" menandakan posisi nuanced.

Tips SNBT: Advanced Reading
Strategi menjawab soal reading comprehension lanjutan
1
Baca pertanyaan dulu. Sebelum membaca teks, lihat pertanyaannya. Ini membantu fokus pada informasi yang dicari.
2
Cari kata kunci penunjuk tone. Kata-kata seperti "unfortunately", "alarmingly", "arguably" menunjukkan sikap penulis. Ini kunci soal inference.
3
"What can be inferred" = cari yang tersirat. Jawaban inference tidak pernah dinyatakan langsung di teks. Carilah apa yang bisa disimpulkan dari bukti yang ada.
4
Perhatikan kata transisi. "However", "nevertheless", "on the other hand" menandakan penulis akan memberikan sudut pandang berlawanan.
5
Eliminate extreme answers. Pilihan jawaban yang terlalu absolut (always, never, completely) biasanya salah. Pilih jawaban yang bernuansa.

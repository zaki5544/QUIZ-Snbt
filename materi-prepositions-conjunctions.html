Prepositions of Time
In, On, At — untuk menunjukkan waktu

Prepositions of time digunakan untuk menunjukkan kapan sesuatu terjadi. Tiga yang paling penting: in, on, dan at.

Preposition Digunakan untuk Contoh
IN Bulan, tahun, musim, waktu lama in January, in 2024, in the morning
ON Hari, tanggal spesifik on Monday, on March 5th, on my birthday
AT Jam, waktu tepat, momen at 3 PM, at noon, at night, at the weekend
Contoh Kalimat
I was born in March on the 15th at 7 AM.
? Saya lahir di bulan Maret, tanggal 15, pukul 7 pagi.
📍
Prepositions of Place
In, On, At — untuk menunjukkan tempat

Prepositions of place menunjukkan di mana sesuatu berada. Aturannya berbeda dari preposition of time.

Preposition Digunakan untuk Contoh
IN Di dalam ruang/area tertutup in the room, in Jakarta, in a car
ON Di atas permukaan on the table, on the wall, on the bus
AT Di titik spesifik at school, at the door, at the bus stop
⬆️
Above / Over
Di atas (tanpa sentuhan). The picture is above the fireplace.
⬇️
Below / Under
Di bawah. The cat is under the table.
📚
Between / Among
Di antara (2 hal vs 3+ hal). Between you and me.
➡️
Next to / Beside
Di samping. She sat beside me.
🔀
Prepositions of Movement & Direction
To, from, through, into, across, along

Prepositions ini menunjukkan arah atau pergerakan dari satu tempat ke tempat lain.

📚
To
Menuju. I go to school every day.
⬅️
From
Dari. She came from Japan.
📚
Into
Masuk ke dalam. He walked into the room.
🛤️
Through
Melewati. We drove through the tunnel.
📚
Across
Menyeberangi. She swam across the river.
🛣️
Along
Sepanjang. They walked along the beach.
📚
Coordinating Conjunctions
FANBOYS — menghubungkan kalimat setara

Coordinating conjunctions menghubungkan dua kalimat atau frasa yang setara. Ingat akronim FANBOYS!

🔤
For
Karena (formal). She left, for she was tired.
?
And
Dan / tambahan. I like coffee and tea.
?
Nor
Dan juga tidak. He doesn't eat nor drink.
🔀
But
Tapi / kontras. She is smart but lazy.
?
Or
Atau / pilihan. Do you want rice or noodles?
📌
Yet
Namun / kontras. It's simple yet effective.
📎
So
Jadi / akibat. I was tired, so I slept.
📚
FANBOYS: For, And, Nor, But, Or, Yet, So. Gunakan koma sebelum conjunction jika menghubungkan dua independent clauses.
📚
Subordinating Conjunctions
Because, although, when, if, while, since...

Subordinating conjunctions menghubungkan dependent clause (anak kalimat) dengan independent clause (induk kalimat). Dependent clause tidak bisa berdiri sendiri.

❓
Sebab: because, since, as
I stayed home because it was raining.
📚
Kontras: although, even though
Although she was tired, she kept studying.
⏰
Waktu: when, while, after, before
While I was cooking, the phone rang.
📚
Syarat: if, unless, provided
If you study hard, you will pass.
Contoh Kalimat Interaktif
Although she studied hard, she didn't pass the exam.
→ Meskipun dia belajar keras, dia tidak lulus ujian.
⚠️
"Although" vs "But" — Jangan gunakan keduanya dalam satu kalimat! "Although she was tired, but she kept working" ?. Pilih salah satu: "Although she was tired, she kept working" ? atau "She was tired, but she kept working" ?.
🔄
Correlative Conjunctions
Pasangan conjunction: both...and, either...or, neither...nor

Correlative conjunctions selalu muncul berpasangan dan menghubungkan elemen yang sejajar (parallel structure).

➕
Both...and
Keduanya. Both Java and Python are popular.
?
Either...or
Salah satu. Either you come or I'll leave.
?
Neither...nor
Tidak keduanya. Neither he nor she agreed.
📚
Not only...but also
Tidak hanya...tapi juga. Not only smart but also kind.
🎯
Tips & Jebakan Soal SNBT
Strategi menjawab soal preposition & conjunction
1
In/On/At punya aturan berbeda untuk waktu dan tempat. "At school" (tempat) vs "at 3 PM" (waktu). Hafalkan konteksnya, bukan terjemahan langsung.
2
Although ≠ But. Jangan gabungkan keduanya. Ini salah satu kesalahan paling umum pelajar bahasa Inggris dan sering jadi jebakan soal.
3
Because vs Because of. Because + clause (S+V). Because of + noun phrase. "Because of the rain" ? vs "Because it rained" ?.
4
Dependent prepositions. Banyak verb/adjective punya preposition tetap: "interested in", "good at", "depend on". Tidak bisa diganti sesuka hati.
5
Neither...nor — verb mengikuti subjek terdekat. "Neither she nor I am guilty." bukan "Neither she nor I is guilty." Aturan proximity agreement.

Artikel 1: The Rise of Artificial Intelligence
Berita tentang perkembangan teknologi
Medium
The Rise of Artificial Intelligence
Artificial Intelligence (AI) has rapidly transformed from a futuristic concept into a daily reality. Across various industries, AI systems are now handling complex tasks with unprecedented efficiency. For instance, in healthcare, machine learning algorithms can analyze medical images to detect diseases earlier than human doctors. This technological breakthrough has significantly improved patient outcomes.

However, the rapid adoption of AI is not without its challenges. There are growing concerns regarding job displacement as automated systems begin to replace human workers in manufacturing and customer service sectors. Furthermore, experts warn about the ethical implications of AI, particularly concerning data privacy and algorithmic bias.

Despite these concerns, most analysts agree that AI will continue to be a driving force in the global economy. The focus now is on regulating its development to ensure it benefits society as a whole while mitigating potential risks.
Pertanyaan Pemahaman
Pertanyaan 1
What is the main idea of the passage?
✅ Jawaban B. Ide utama teks ini adalah perkembangan AI yang cepat membawa manfaat yang signifikan namun juga tantangan.
Pertanyaan 2
According to the text, how does AI help in healthcare?
? Jawaban C. Teks menyebutkan bahwa AI membantu menganalisis gambar medis untuk mendeteksi penyakit lebih awal (paragraf satu).
Pertanyaan 3
What is the author's tone in the last paragraph?
✅ Jawaban A. Penulis bersikap objektif dan pragmatis, menyimpulkan bahwa meskipun ada masalah, AI akan terus berkembang dan regulasi diperlukan.

Artikel 2: Global Climate Crisis
Artikel tentang krisis lingkungan global
Medium
Global Climate Crisis
The impact of global climate change is becoming increasingly evident across the planet. Rising global temperatures have led to the rapid melting of polar ice caps, resulting in a dangerous rise in sea levels. Coastal cities worldwide are now facing the imminent threat of severe flooding and storm surges.

Beyond rising sea levels, climate change is causing more frequent and intense extreme weather events. For example, prolonged droughts are devastating agricultural yields in several regions, sparking fears of widespread food insecurity. Conversely, other areas are experiencing unprecedented rainfall leading to devastating landslides and urban flooding.

Environmental scientists stress that immediate and coordinated international action is required to cut greenhouse gas emissions. Transitioning to renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, is cited as a crucial step in mitigating further environmental degradation.
Pertanyaan Pemahaman
Pertanyaan 1
What is a direct consequence of the melting polar ice caps according to the text?
✅ Jawaban B. Teks secara eksplisit menyebutkan "melting of polar ice caps, resulting in a dangerous rise in sea levels."
Pertanyaan 2
Based on the passage, what can be inferred about the impact of climate change on food supply?
? Jawaban C. Teks menyebutkan bahwa kekeringan panjang merusak hasil pertanian, yang memicu "fears of widespread food insecurity."
Pertanyaan 3
What solution is highlighted by environmental scientists in the final paragraph?
✅ Jawaban D. "Transitioning to renewable energy sources... is cited as a crucial step."

Artikel 3: The Psychology of Social Media
Artikel tentang psikologi dunia maya
Medium
The Psychology of Social Media
Social media platforms have revolutionized how we communicate, yet their psychological impact remains a subject of intense debate. Design features such as infinite scrolling and notification badges are engineered to trigger the brain's reward system, releasing dopamine. This mechanism often leads to compulsive behavior, with users checking their devices incessantly.

Moreover, psychological studies highlight the negative correlation between heavy social media usage and mental well-being. The culture of curated reality online encourages detrimental social comparisons. Users frequently compare their behind-the-scenes struggles with the highlight reels of their peers, fostering feelings of inadequacy and anxiety.

However, it is vital to acknowledge the positive aspects as well. For marginalized groups and isolated individuals, these platforms provide crucial communities and support networks that might be unavailable locally. Therefore, managing social media consumption, rather than complete avoidance, is widely recommended by psychologists.
Pertanyaan Pemahaman
Pertanyaan 1
Why do social media design features like infinite scrolling lead to compulsive behavior?
✅ Jawaban A. Teks menyebutkan fitur tersebut "trigger the brain's reward system, releasing dopamine" yang memicu perilaku kompulsif.
Pertanyaan 2
What does the term "curated reality" in paragraph 2 most closely refer to?
? Jawaban B. "Curated reality" atau realitas yang dikurasi merujuk pada kebiasaan pengguna yang hanya memposting bagian-bagian terbaik dari hidup mereka ("highlight reels").
Pertanyaan 3
What is the author's overall stance on social media usage?
✅ Jawaban C. Penulis menyoroti sisi negatif dan positif, lalu menyimpulkan "managing social media consumption... is widely recommended."

Tips Membaca untuk SNBT
Strategi menjawab soal reading comprehension
1
Baca pertanyaan dulu. Sebelum membaca teks, lihat dulu pertanyaannya. Ini membuat otakmu "tahu apa yang dicari" saat membaca.
2
Jangan terjemahkan kata per kata. Fokus pada pemahaman umum. Kamu tidak perlu memahami setiap kata untuk menjawab pertanyaan.
3
Perhatikan kata kunci di pertanyaan. Who, what, when, where, why, how — kata-kata ini memberi petunjuk tentang informasi yang harus dicari.
4
Cari jawaban di teks, bukan dari asumsi. Jawaban yang benar selalu didukung oleh informasi yang ada di teks. Jangan menebak dari pengetahuan umum.
5
Perhatikan sinonim. Jawaban di pilihan ganda sering menggunakan kata yang berbeda tapi artinya sama dengan yang ada di teks (parafrase).

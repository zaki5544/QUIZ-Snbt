Anatomi Kalimat yang Baik
Komponen dasar kalimat bahasa Inggris

Setiap kalimat yang benar harus memiliki minimal subject + verb. Kalimat yang baik juga harus jelas, ringkas, dan memiliki satu ide utama.

1️⃣
Simple Sentence
Satu independent clause. She studies every day.
2️⃣
Compound Sentence
Dua independent clauses + conjunction. She studies hard, and she gets good grades.
3️⃣
Complex Sentence
Independent + dependent clause. Because she studies hard, she gets good grades.
4️⃣
Compound-Complex
Kombinasi ketiganya. Although she is busy, she studies hard, and she gets good grades.
✏️
Kesalahan Kalimat yang Umum
Run-on, fragment, dan cara memperbaikinya

Tiga kesalahan paling umum dalam menulis kalimat bahasa Inggris:

❌ Run-on Sentence (kalimat terlalu panjang tanpa pemisah)
I love reading I go to the library every day.
✅ Perbaikan: I love reading, so I go to the library every day.
❌ Sentence Fragment (kalimat tidak lengkap)
Because she was tired.
✅ Perbaikan: Because she was tired, she went to bed early.
❌ Comma Splice (koma tanpa conjunction)
He is smart, he studies hard.
✅ Perbaikan: He is smart, and he studies hard.
📚
Struktur Paragraf
Topic sentence ? supporting ? concluding

Paragraf yang baik memiliki tiga komponen utama:

📚
Topic Sentence
Kalimat pertama yang menyatakan ide utama paragraf. Ini adalah peta bagi pembaca.
🟢
Supporting Sentences
Kalimat-kalimat yang mendukung, menjelaskan, atau memberikan contoh untuk ide utama.
📚
Concluding Sentence
Kalimat penutup yang merangkum atau memberikan transisi ke paragraf berikutnya.
Contoh Paragraf yang Baik
🔵 Reading is one of the most effective ways to improve English skills. Regular reading exposes learners to new vocabulary in context, helping them understand word usage naturally. Additionally, it improves grammar intuition — readers subconsciously absorb correct sentence structures through repeated exposure. Studies show that students who read for at least 30 minutes daily score significantly higher on language proficiency tests. 🟡 Therefore, incorporating daily reading into your study routine can dramatically accelerate your English learning journey.

Transition Words & Cohesion
Menghubungkan kalimat dan paragraf

Transition words membuat tulisanmu mengalir dan logis. Tanpa transisi, paragraf terasa seperti daftar kalimat yang tidak terhubung.

➕
Addition
Furthermore, Moreover, In addition, Additionally, Also
📚
Contrast
However, Nevertheless, On the other hand, Although, Despite
➡️
Cause/Effect
Therefore, Consequently, As a result, Thus, Hence
📌
Example
For example, For instance, Such as, Specifically, To illustrate
📚
Conclusion
In conclusion, To sum up, Overall, In summary, Ultimately
📊
Sequence
First, Second, Next, Then, Finally, Subsequently
📚
Tips Menulis yang Efektif
Prinsip-prinsip writing yang baik
1
Satu paragraf = satu ide utama. Jangan campur beberapa topik dalam satu paragraf. Jika berganti topik, mulai paragraf baru.
2
Variasikan panjang kalimat. Campurkan kalimat pendek dan panjang untuk ritme yang menarik. Kalimat pendek memberi dampak. Kalimat panjang menjelaskan detail.
3
Show, don't tell. Alih-alih "The city is beautiful", tulis "Golden sunlight reflected off the glass towers as cherry blossoms danced in the spring breeze."
4
Hindari passive voice berlebihan. Active voice lebih kuat dan langsung. "The team completed the project" vs "The project was completed by the team."
5
Proofread! Selalu baca ulang tulisanmu. Cek ejaan, grammar, dan apakah setiap kalimat berkontribusi pada ide utama paragraf.
